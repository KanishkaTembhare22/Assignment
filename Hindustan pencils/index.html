The Most Popular Pencil of India

Hindustan Pencils is India's largest pencil manufacturer
with high-quality products being delivered to
over 50 countries worldwide. Our state-of-the-art
manufacturing units with the latest machinery set up daily
produce 8.5 million pencils, 1.7 million sharpeners,
2.7 million erasers, 0.3 million scales, 1 million pens.
